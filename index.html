✦ The Beginning

Chapter One
a tiny story

Once upon a very normal day…
two people started talking.

One of them was Namrata.
The other was simply a boy.

Nothing dramatic.
No violins playing.
No movie scene.

Just… two people.

✦ The Confession

And then…
Namrata did something brave.

She told the boy she liked him.

That kind of honesty is rare.

✦ The Honest Problem

And the boy…

…didn't want to lie.

So he thought carefully.

Because honesty matters more than pretending.

✦ The Honest Answer

The boy thought about it.
He realized something important.

"I really like talking to you."
"I like your presence."
"I like the way you are."

But love…

…is something he didn't want to fake.

Because Namrata deserved the real version of it.

✦ The Future

Stories are strange.

Sometimes the brave character
appears earlier than expected.

And in this story…
that character was Namrata.

· · ·

She said what she felt.

She didn't hide it.
She didn't pretend.

That kind of honesty takes courage.

And courage is rare.

Whether this story becomes a friendship,
something more later,
or just a really nice chapter…

the boy is genuinely glad
Namrata showed up in it.

Chapter one was already
worth writing.
